About Me

Background

I'm a Web Developer with a passion and commitment to connecting and improving the world. As a problem-solver and lover of challenges, I enjoy the thrill of learning and the efficiency with which abstract ideas can be brought to life.

Originally from the good ole’ Midwest, I graduated from Dartmouth College with a B.A. in Mathematics. It was in college when I began my exploration of programming by taking numerous Computer Science and Engineering courses. In fact, one of my most vivid memories from college is learning about and implementing “linked lists,” as I was in awe of how entire lists could be constructed and manipulated by storing simple pointers. I became instantly hooked.

After college, I came to the New York City area (including New Jersey) to finally pursue my programming passion full-time. I look forward to the many more problems to solve, ideas to bring to life, and people to be reached through technology.
